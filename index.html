Biography

I was a Senior Applied Scientist at JD.COM, selected as a member of the Tech Genius Team (TGT). My recent work focuses on Large Language Models and AI Agents, specifically in LLM post-training and multi-agent systems.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in June 2024, advised by Prof. Zhenan Sun on 3D vision and biometrics. Prior to that, I earned my M.S. degree in Computer Science from the Institute of Software, CAS (ISCAS) in 2020, and my B.E. in Software Engineering & B.A. in Japanese dual degree from Dalian University of Technology (DLUT).

Up to now, I have published and submitted more than 20 papers, including 10 first-authored, across Top-tier Conferences, JCR-Q1 journals, and the Best Student Paper Award. The multi-agent framework (OxyGent) I contributed to has garnered over 2,000 GitHub stars. The open-source computer vision benchmarks (CASIA-Iris-Degradation and CASIA-Iris-Africa) I constructed have been downloaded over 1,000 times.
